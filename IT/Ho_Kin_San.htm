Proposal of IT service team・s competitions

2003-2004

Background

Our aim is to increase opportunities for our students to use computers, and know more about IT. We are supposed to hold some activities and competitions in order to widen our schoolmate・s knowledge in IT area.

Objective

The competitions aim at increasing student・s chance to use computers, different software and involve more in school activities. All schoolmates are welcome to participate in competitions we organize. Therefore, it will eventually improve our students・ interest in IT.

Competitions

There will be 3-4 competitions.

A.     X・mas card/red packet /e-Card design competition in November or December

Since it is near Christmas and lunar New Year, a design competition can be held. Students can design x・mas card, red packet or e-card to join the competition. They can use different software to design their ideal card, but the format of their design must be in .jpg or .bmp etc. Through the competition, they can know how to use and what kind of graphical software can be used for designing. The IT chairperson and the IT teachers will become the judges. They will decide who the winner is.

B.     Inter-class IT competition in the post exam period

All classes will be allowed to join the competition. If there are many classes are willing to join the competition, we will divide the competition into first round, second round, semi-final, and final etc. Only those classes which participate well can enter the final. The final will be held at school hall with our students and our IT teachers to be the judges. Prize will be given to the champion. The runner-up will also have the prize. Through the Inter-class IT competition, student can get the latest news of computers and technology. Since all students can participate into the competition, they can enjoy the game and with many fun.

*C. Animation design competition

After IT service team finish the teaching of ．・Flash lesson・・, the students can join the followed competition to use the flash skills which learnt from those lessons. They can use flash or any other animation design software to design their work. All students will be welcome to join the competition. The animation should not be longer than 1 min. Our teachers will become the judges and prize will be given to the winner.

*D. Small quiz in the IT express

We can use a small cornea of the IT express and put a small quiz with a few questions which are about IT and let our readers answer them. Small prize will be given to those readers which get all or most answers correct. 

Budget

The proposed budget for each competition is around HKD$200-$300. It will include the prizes for those winners. (By the way, I am not sure what we will buy for the prize, so there is only a guess budget. The budget may be smaller.)
